How It Works

1. Submit a Report

Fill out our simple form with details about the issue you've noticed in one of our local socity.

2. Authorities Review

We forward your report to the relevant park authorities who assess and prioritize the issue.

3. Track Resolution

Follow the progress of your reported issue from submission to resolution with our tracking system.

Broken Swing at Playground

In Progress

One of the swings at the children's playground area has a broken chain that needs to be replaced for safety.

Riverside Park Reported: May 12, 2023

Authority Response: Maintenance team scheduled for repair on May 18. Parts have been ordered.

Overflowing Trash Bins

Resolved

The trash bins near the picnic area are overflowing and attracting wildlife. Need more frequent collection.

Oakwood Community Park Reported: May 8, 2023

Authority Response: Trash collection schedule increased to twice daily. Area has been cleaned.

Fallen Tree Blocking Trail

Pending

A large tree has fallen across the main hiking trail after recent storms. Trail is currently impassable.

Pinecrest Nature Reserve Reported: May 15, 2023

Authority Response: Report received. Assessment team will evaluate within 48 hours.

Graffiti on Restroom Building

Resolved

Inappropriate graffiti has appeared on the exterior walls of the park restroom facilities.

Riverside Park Reported: May 5, 2023

Authority Response: Graffiti removed and area repainted. Security patrols increased.

Success Stories

Playground Renovation

Riverside Park

Multiple reports about outdated and unsafe playground equipment led to a complete renovation of the children's play area. New, accessible equipment was installed within 60 days of the initial reports.

12 citizen reports contributed to this improvement

Trail Maintenance & Signage

Pinecrest Nature Reserve

Feedback about confusing trail markers and poor drainage areas prompted a comprehensive trail improvement project. New signs, maps, and drainage solutions were implemented.

8 citizen reports contributed to this improvement
